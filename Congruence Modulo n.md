==Congruence Modulo n== - [[Equivalence Relation]] such:
$a ≡ b$ (mod $n$), read, “==$a$ is congruent to $b$ modulo $n$.==” 
For example, we have $15 ≡ 27$ (mod $4$) because both $15$ and $27$ have remainder $3$ when divided by $4$.

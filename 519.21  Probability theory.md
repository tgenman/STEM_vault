- $\Omega \in F$

- $A_1, A_2,... \in F \Rightarrow A_1 \cup A_2 \cup ... \in F$

- $P(\Omega) = 1$
- $A_1, A_2,... \in F$ are disjoint sets $\Rightarrow P(A_1 \sqcup A_2 \sqcup ...) = \sum_{i=1}^{\infty} P(A_i)$

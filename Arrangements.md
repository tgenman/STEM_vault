Let $A = \set{a_1,..., a_n}$

An ==arrangement== or ==permutation== of $n$ elements (of the set A) is an ordered n-tuple $(x_1,..., x_n)$ elements of A, where each elements occurs exactly once

No Repetition

$$
A_n = n \cdot (n-1) \cdot ... \cdot 1 = n!
$$
With Repetition
$$
A_n = n \cdot n \cdot ... \cdot n = n^n
$$

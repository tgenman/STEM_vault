> [!tip] Cycle $C_n {}$ (n-cycle)
> is a graph of order ${} n {}$ and size $m {}$ whose vertices can be labeled by ${} v_1, v_2, . . . , v_n {}$ and whose edges are ${} v_1v_n {}$ and ${} v_iv_{i+1} {}$ for $i = 1,2,...,n−1$ (for an integer ${} n ≥ 3 {}$)

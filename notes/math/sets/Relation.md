==A relation== between [[Set (math)|sets]] $A$ and $B$ is a subset $\mathscr{R}$ of $A\times B$ ([[Cartesian product of sets]]). 
We read $(a,b)∈R$ as “a is related to b” and write $a$ $\mathscr{R}$ $b$.

> [!tip] Independence
Two events $A$ and $B$ are said to be independent if 
> $P(A \cap B) = P(A)P(B)$
> $P(A | B) = P(A)$ if  $P(B) > 0$

If $A$ and $B$ are independent, so are $A$ and $B^c$.

==Independence== does not imply [[Conditional independence|conditional independence]] , and vice versa.

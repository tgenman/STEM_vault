Let $X, X_1, X_2, \ldots, X_n$ be i.i.d. r.v., $\mu = \mathbb{E}[X]$ and $\sigma^2 = \text{Var}[X]$.

Laws of large numbers (weak and strong): $$ \bar{X}_n := \frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^n X_i \xrightarrow{\mathbb{P}, a.s.} \mu \text{ as } n \rightarrow \infty. $$

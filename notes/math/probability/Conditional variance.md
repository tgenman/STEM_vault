-  $V[Y | X] = \mathbb{E}[(Y - \mathbb{E}[Y | X])^2 | X] = \mathbb{E}[Y^2 | X] - (\mathbb{E}[Y | X])^2$.
- $V[Y] = \mathbb{E}[V [Y | X]] + V[\mathbb{E}[Y | X]]$

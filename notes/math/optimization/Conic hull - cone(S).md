> [!tip] Conic hull of $S$
is set of all [[Conic combination]]  of points in set $S$:
$\mathbf{cone}(S) = \left\{ \sum\limits_{i=1}^k\theta_i x_i \mid x_i \in S, \; \theta_i \ge 0\right\}$

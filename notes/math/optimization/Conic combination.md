> [!tip] Conic combination  
for $x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_k \in S$ and $\theta_1, \theta_2, \ldots, \theta_k \in \mathbb{R}$  
is the point $\theta_1 x_1 + \theta_2 x_2 + \ldots + \theta_k x_k$  
if $\theta_i \geq 0$ for all $i$.

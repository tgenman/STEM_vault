> [!tip] Adjacent vertices
> If $uv$ is an edge of $G$, then $u$ and $v$ are adjacent vertices.

> [!tip] Adjacent edges
> If $uv$ and $vw$ are distinct edges in $G$, then $uv$ and $vw$ are adjacent edges.

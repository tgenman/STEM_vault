> [!tip] Neighbours
> Two [[Adjacent in graph|adjacent vertices]] are referred to as neighbours of each other.

> [!tip] Open neighborhood of ${} v {}$ ($N_G(v) {}$, or $N(v)$)
> The set of neighbours of a vertex $v {}$

> [!tip] Closed neighborhood of $v$
> The set ${} N[v] = N(v) ∪ {v}$

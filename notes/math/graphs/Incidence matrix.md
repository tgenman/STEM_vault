> [!tip] The incidence matrix of G 
is the $n×m$ matrix $M_G := (m_{ve}) {}$, where $m_ve$ is the number of times ($0, 1$, or $2$) that vertex $v$ and edge $e$ are [[Incident vertex and edge|incident]] .

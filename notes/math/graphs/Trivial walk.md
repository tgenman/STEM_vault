> [!tip] Trivial walk
is walk consist of a single vertex 

A trivial walk is therefore a [[Closed walk]] closed walk.


A nontrivial closed walk in a graph G in which no edge is repeated is a circuit in G. 
For example,
$$C = (u,w,x,y,w,v,u)$$

> [!tip] Multigraph $H$
> is a nonempty set of vertices, every two of which are joined by a finite number of edges. Hence a multigraph ${} H$ may be expressed as ${} H = (V,E)$, where $E$ is a multiset of $2$-element subsets of $V$

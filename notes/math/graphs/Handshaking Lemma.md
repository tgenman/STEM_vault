> [!info] Handshaking Lemma
> If $G$ is a graph of size $m$, then
> $$\sum_{v\in V(G)}\deg v=2m.$$

### Proof
When summing the degrees of the vertices of $G$, each edge of $G$ is counted twice, once for each of its two incident vertices.

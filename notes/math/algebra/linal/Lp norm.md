> [!tip] $\ell_p$-norm
In general, for $x = [x_1, \ldots, x_n] \in \mathbb{R}^n$ an $\ell_p$-norm is defined as follows:
$\|x\|_p = \left( \sum_{i=1}^n |x_i|^p \right)^{\frac{1}{p}}.$

| ------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :---------------------- |

| 1       | $\ell_1$ - [[Manhattan norm (L1)]] $\|\cdot\|_1$; <br>[[Среднее арифметическое]] | [[Expected Value E(X)]] |

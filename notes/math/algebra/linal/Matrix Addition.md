The sum of two matrices $A \in \mathbb{R}^{m \times n}$, $B \in \mathbb{R}^{m \times n}$ is defined as the element-wise sum, i.e.,

$$
A + B :=
\left[
\begin{matrix}
a_{11} + b_{11} & \cdots & a_{1n} + b_{1n} \\
\vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\
a_{m1} + b_{m1} & \cdots & a_{mn} + b_{mn}
\end{matrix}
\right]
\in \mathbb{R}^{m \times n}.
$$

> [!tip] A function is bijective 
if it is both [[Injective mapping property|injective]] and [[Surjective mapping property|surjective]].
 $\forall a \in A \exists b \in B: f(a) = b$ и $\forall b \in B \exists a \in A: f(b) = a$.

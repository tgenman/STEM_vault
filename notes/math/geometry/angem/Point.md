#анки

Let $n$ be a [[Natural numbers N|positive whole number]] . We define

$R^n =$ all ordered $n-tuples$ of [[Real numbers R|Real Numbers]] $(x_1,x_2,x_3,...,x_n)$.

An $n-tuple$ of real numbers is called a _point_ of $R^n$.

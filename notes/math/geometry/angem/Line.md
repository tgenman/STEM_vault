#анки

_line_ is a ray that is _straight_ and _infinite_ in both directions.

### implicit equation
$$
\Large x + y = 1
$$
### parametric form
$$
\Large l: \{ (x, y, z) \in R^3 | (x, y, z) = p_0 + t \vec{v}, t \in R \}
$$
### Symmetric form
$$
\large  x = p_{ox} + tv_x
$$

$$
\Large l: \{ \frac{x - p_{ox}}{v_x} = \frac{x - p_{oy}}{v_y} = \frac{x - p_{oz}}{v_z} \}
$$

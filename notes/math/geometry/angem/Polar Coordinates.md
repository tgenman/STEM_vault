Adding any **even** multiple of $π$ to $a$ gives us the **same** result.

Adding any **odd** multiple of $π$ to $a$ and **reversing** the sign of $r$ gives us the same result.

$$
y = r \sin \theta
$$
$$
x = r \cos \theta
$$
$$
r = \sqrt{x^2 + y^2}
$$
$$
\theta = \tan^{-1} \frac{y}{x}
$$

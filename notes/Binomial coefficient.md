$$
\binom{n}{k} = \frac{n!}{k!(n-k)!}
$$

---
$$
\binom{0}{0} = 1
$$
$$
\binom{1}{0} = 1
$$

$$
\binom{n}{0} = 1
$$
$$
\binom{n}{1} = n
$$

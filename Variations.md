Let $k \leq n$  and $A = \set{a_1,..., a_n}$ be a finite set

A ==k-variation== from n-elements (from n-element set A) is an [[Arrangements]] of some of k-element subset of A

No repetition:
$$
V_{n, k} = \frac{n!}{(n-k)!} = n \cdot (n-1) \cdot ... \cdot (n - k + 1)
$$
